Chicken and Broccoli Alfredo

Description

Easy and delicious pasta recipe for broccoli lovers

Original recipe from Tasty.co

Ingredients

  • Penne pasta
  • Broccoli
  • Chicken breasts
  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Spices (salt, black pepper, nutmeg, red pepper flake)

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in salted water for 8 minutes
  2. Add broccoli to pasta and simmer for 4 minutes with the lid on
  3. Strain the pasta and broccoli in a colander
  4. Heat the olive oil in the pot over medium-high heat, and add the chicken. Season the chicken with season salt and pepper
  5. Once the chicken is cooked through, remove it from the pot and set aside.
  6. Add the butter to the pot, and melt it. Once butter is fully melted, add in garlic and stir continuously for 10 seconds
  7. Add the heavy cream, salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Stir often, and cook until sauce starts to bubble slightly
  8. Add in Parmesan cheese and stir until the sauce thickens
  9. Add in the cooked chicken, pasta, and broccoli, and stir until fully combined.
  10. Top with Parmesan cheese or red chili flakes
